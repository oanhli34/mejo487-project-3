A Deep-Dive into the Metropolitan Museum of Art's Collections

By Oanh Nguyen
November 5th, 2024

The Metropolitan Museum of Art ("The Met") is the largest art museum within the Americas. The museum provides select datasets on more than 470,000 artworks in its collection for unrestricted use by waiving all rights possible under the law regarding copyright of the dataset. The Met intends for these datasets to support the research, usage, and interaction with its collection.
